Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Prime Meridian AI is a sole trader business operated by Kris Farebrother, based in the United Kingdom. We provide AI audit and automation strategy services for owner-managed businesses.

Contact: kris@primemeridianai.com

What data we collect

When you visit our website

We use Apollo.io to identify businesses visiting our website. This may associate your visit with your company based on IP address and publicly available business data. This is used solely to understand which types of businesses are interested in our services. No personal browsing history is tracked or stored by us.

When you complete a form on our website

Our waitlist and enquiry forms collect your name, work email address, company name, and other details you choose to provide (such as company size, industry, and role). This data may be enriched using Apollo.io with publicly available business information associated with your email domain — such as company size, industry, and location. We do not enrich personal data such as phone numbers without your explicit consent.

When you book a discovery call

We use Calendly to manage bookings. When you book a call, Calendly collects your name and email address. This is governed by Calendly's privacy policy.

When we contact you about our services

We may contact business professionals by email to introduce our services, where we have a legitimate interest in doing so under UK GDPR. We use publicly available business contact information sourced through Apollo.io. Every outreach email includes a clear and immediate way to opt out. If you ask us not to contact you, we will stop immediately and suppress your details from future outreach.

During an AI Audit engagement

Stakeholder interviews are recorded with your consent and transcribed using AI transcription services (Fireflies.ai). Transcripts are stored securely and used only for analysis during the audit. They are not shared with third parties. After the audit report is delivered, you can request deletion of all interview data at any time.

How we use your data

  • To respond to your enquiries and form submissions
  • To contact business professionals who may benefit from our services (legitimate interest basis)
  • To deliver the AI Audit service if you become a client
  • To understand which types of businesses visit our website

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal basis for processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent — when you submit a form, book a call, or agree to interview recording
  • Legitimate interest — for B2B outreach to business professionals and website visitor identification, where we have assessed that our interest does not override your rights and freedoms
  • Contract — to deliver services you have engaged us to provide

Third-party services

We use the following services to operate this website and our business:

  • Apollo.io — website visitor identification, form enrichment, and B2B prospecting (privacy policy)
  • Calendly — appointment scheduling (privacy policy)
  • Attio — customer relationship management (privacy policy)
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting (privacy policy)
  • Fireflies.ai — meeting transcription for audit clients (privacy policy)

Cookies and tracking

Our website uses tracking technology provided by Apollo.io to identify visiting businesses. This may set cookies or use similar technologies to associate visits with company information. If you interact with embedded third-party content (such as the Calendly booking widget), those services may set their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for email marketing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, email kris@primemeridianai.com.

Data retention

Form submission data is retained until you request deletion. Prospect contact data sourced for B2B outreach is retained until you opt out or request deletion, at which point your details are suppressed from future contact. AI Audit interview recordings and transcripts are retained for 12 months after delivery unless you request earlier deletion. Business correspondence is retained for up to 6 years for legal and accounting purposes.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent version.
