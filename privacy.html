Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Prime Meridian AI is a sole trader business operated by Kris Farebrother, based in the United Kingdom. We provide AI audit and automation strategy services for owner-managed businesses.

Contact: kris@primemeridianai.com

What data we collect

When you download the Mini AI Self-Audit guide

We collect your name and email address (required), and optionally your company name, company size, company website, and phone number. This data is stored securely in Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), our email marketing platform.

When you book a discovery call

We use Calendly to manage bookings. When you book a call, Calendly collects your name and email address. This is governed by Calendly's privacy policy.

During an AI Audit engagement

Stakeholder interviews are recorded with your consent and transcribed using AI transcription services (Fireflies.ai). Transcripts are stored securely and used only for analysis during the audit. They are not shared with third parties. After the audit report is delivered, you can request deletion of all interview data at any time.

Website analytics

We use GoatCounter for website analytics. GoatCounter is a privacy-friendly analytics tool that does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track individual users. It tells us which pages are visited and from which countries — nothing more.

How we use your data

  • To send you the guide you requested
  • To send occasional updates about AI strategy for professional services (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To deliver the AI Audit service if you become a client
  • To respond to your enquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

We use the following services to operate this website and our business:

  • Brevo — email marketing and form handling (privacy policy)
  • Calendly — appointment scheduling (privacy policy)
  • GoatCounter — privacy-friendly website analytics (privacy policy)
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting (privacy policy)
  • Fireflies.ai — meeting transcription for audit clients (privacy policy)

Cookies

This website does not use cookies. GoatCounter, our analytics tool, does not set cookies. If you interact with embedded third-party content (such as the Calendly booking widget), those services may set their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for email marketing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, email kris@primemeridianai.com.

Data retention

Email subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. AI Audit interview recordings and transcripts are retained for 12 months after delivery unless you request earlier deletion. Business correspondence is retained for up to 6 years for legal and accounting purposes.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent version.
