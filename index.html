The Insider Method

How the AI Audit works

Three steps. No jargon. Built around how your business actually operates.

1

We talk to your people

Short, structured conversations with you and key team members about their actual day-to-day work. What takes too long, what falls through the cracks, what's repetitive. No questionnaires — real conversations.

3-5 interviews, 30 mins each
2

We map every opportunity

Using AI-assisted analysis of those conversations, we identify every place where AI or automation could genuinely help — and every risk to watch for. Each opportunity is scored on two dimensions: impact and effort.

Analysis takes 3-5 working days
3

You get a prioritised map

A clear, visual report showing every opportunity plotted on an impact-vs-effort matrix. Start with the high-impact, low-effort wins. Work outward at your own pace. Act on what makes sense — with whoever you choose.

Delivered as a written report + walkthrough call
